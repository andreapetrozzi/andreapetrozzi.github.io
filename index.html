About Me

Hi! I am a Front-End Developer jr. and UI/UX Designer with many years of experience in the field of communication and the web. In 2014 I graduated in Applied Psychological Sciences.

After completing my studies, I decided to try my hand and put into practice ideas that had long accompanied me throughout my academic career: to become an author of illustrated novels. Passion that still today in my free time does not give up and that has been fundamental to personal and work development.

During my career, since 2016 I have collaborated for different realities (brand and architecture studio, agency for PA and SMEs) acquiring over the years, both a methodology aimed at the sector, and a growing interest in the use of new professional tools and software. I have always loved to range and have as wide a range of interests as possible. My passions range from travel, art (in all its forms), literature, writing, history, chess, to neuro-marketing and social psychology. Each cultivated passion has always fueled and stimulated my curiosity by increasing its creative vision, with the intention and goal of addressing every problem in the world of work with originality and recognition, trying to find new solutions and development horizons.

Interested in graphics and above all in new information technologies (which allow to give space and centrality in our lives), from a path, started as communication and marketing, I arrived at web design and code development, trying to deepen every aspect related to functionality and “latest generation creativity”. In recent years I have specialized in Web Design as a developer of WP sites and, with the development of the Front-End, I was finally able to complete my training circle. The purpose and objective that I would like to pursue in the future both in work (and in life) is to convey both my academic background and coding as a fundamental (logistical and operational) basis in establishing a creative and distinctive approach in the phase design and development of new applications, and thus be able to meet questions and needs not yet known or not yet explicit. What has always fascinated me is (and will be) the care, study, design and creation of the Visual aspect of applications and sites (UI and UX in large part), always experimenting with new innovative and creative ideas. from a logistical and operational point of view.

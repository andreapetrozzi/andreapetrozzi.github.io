About Me

Hi! I'm Andrea and I'm a Front-End Developer jr. and UI-UX Designer with many years of experience in the field of communication and the web.

After graduating in Applied Psychological Sciences, I decided to try my hand and realize ideas that had long accompanied me during my studies: to become a Graphic Designer an author of illustrated novels. Passion that still today has been fundamental for personal and work development.

Since 2016 I have collaborated for various activities, acquiring over the years both a methodology aimed at the reference sector, and a growing interest in the use of new professional tools and software. I have always loved to range and have the widest range of interests possible, helping to feed and stimulate my curiosity by increasing my creative vision.

Interested in the new technologies of the IT field (which allow us to give space and centrality to our life), from a path, which began as communication and marketing, I arrived at web design and code development, trying to deepen every aspect related to functionality and to the “latest generation creativity”. In recent years I have specialized as a developer of WordPress sites, with the development of the Front-End, I was finally able to complete my training path.

What has always fascinated me is the visual care, study, design and creation of applications and sites. The personal goal that I would like to pursue in my work is to transmit my academic background combined with coding in order to have a creative and functional approach in the development phase in being able to answer questions and needs not yet known or not yet explicit.
